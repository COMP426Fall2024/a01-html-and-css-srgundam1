SeasonSummary
Battle for Dream IslandTwenty inanimate objects are introduced as contestants competing for Dream Island. The first challenge is to jump off a cliff, and the contestants are divided into two teams: the Squishy Cherries and the Squashy Grapes. The losing team faces elimination.
Battle for Dream Island AgainThe "winner" is chased by the other contestants after stealing Dream Island. Eventually, the competition is restarted, with new and returning characters participating. This season introduces a different voting system where viewers vote for contestants to join or rejoin the competition.
