Battle for Dream Island (BFDI) is an animated web series set in a fictional world where inanimate objects are personified and compete in various challenges to win a grand prize: Dream Island. The show follows a reality TV-style competition format, where the characters (who are objects like a tennis ball, leaf, pencil, etc.) form alliances, face eliminations, and tackle various challenges. Contestants are eliminated based on votes from real-life viewers.
